Iconic Swiss Destinations

From majestic peaks to serene lakes, explore the diverse beauty that makes Switzerland unforgettable.

Jungfrau Region

Top of Europe

Journey to the highest railway station in Europe at 3,454 meters. Experience breathtaking views of the Aletsch Glacier, the longest glacier in the Alps, and stand atop the world at the Sphinx Observatory.

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Year-round accessibility
  • Spectacular hiking trails
Discover Jungfrau

Lake Geneva

Riviera Elegance

Discover the sophisticated charm of Switzerland's largest lake. From the Olympic capital Lausanne to the jazz haven of Montreux, experience vineyard terraces, Belle Époque grandeur, and Mediterranean-style lakeside promenades.

  • Lavaux vineyard terraces (UNESCO)
  • Chillon Castle medieval fortress
  • World-class cultural events
Explore Lake Geneva

Zermatt

Matterhorn Magic

Stand in awe of the iconic Matterhorn, one of the world's most photographed mountains. This car-free alpine village offers world-class skiing, mountaineering heritage, and an atmosphere that captures the essence of Swiss mountain culture.

  • 365 days of skiing
  • Car-free village atmosphere
  • Gornergrat railway panoramas
Visit Zermatt
